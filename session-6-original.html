Session 6: Artificial Intelligence

 

 

Since becoming an academic field of study in the 1950s, artificial intelligence seeped into numerous Web services. Search engines (like Google Search and Bing) and digital assistants (like Siri and Alexa) are two examples. OpenAI brought artificial intelligence to mainstream attention when it released ChatGPT in November 2022; within two months of it's launch, ChatGTP had more than 100 million monthly users making it the fasted adopted Web service in history. In this session we will survey examples of artificial intelligence found on the Web, review the branches and types of artificial intelligence, and explore AI's perceived benefits and risks to society.
