Session 7: The Dark Web

 

 

Many people imagine that the Dark Web is a mysterious corner of the Internet where illicit activity occurs. In addition to clarifying what and where the Dark Web is, in this session we will examine how it is accessed; examples of Dark Web activities, sites, and services; and the type of Web users often encountered there: hackers.

Discussion Prompts

  1. Using the Dark Web is essentially about maintaining anonymity online. Do you think there are scenarios where being anonymous online is appropriate?
  2. Governments--including our own--are known to use the Dark Web to very broadly monitor the online activities of their citizens. Do you support such activity? Why or why not?
  3. Hackers use the Dark Web to perpetrate cyberattacks. What aspects of your online life are the most important to protect from cyberattacks? What steps do you take to protect those things?
