Session 8: Cybersecurity

 

 

As the Web has advanced, so has the frequency and sophistication of attacks against digital systems by bad actors; malicious hackers use malware (like viruses and ransomware) to compromise systems--sometimes to make them inoperable and sometimes to steal digital data assets. Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, and software systems from digital attacks. In this session, we will review types of malware and bad actors; the cost cyberattacks have on organizations; and steps organizations take to protect themselves from cyberattacks.
